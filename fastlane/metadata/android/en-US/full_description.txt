Weather forecast with widgets for up to 10 days, based on open data from the Deutscher Wetterdienst (DWD).

A forecast ist available for over 5000 stations in Germany and worldwide.

- detailed weather forecast
- slim design
- supports Gadgetbridge (experimental)

Permissions:
- INTERNET: needed to access the open data from the Deutscher Wetterdienst (DWD).
- FOREGROUND_SERVICE: needed to be able to update weather data regularly.
- RECEIVE_BOOT COMPLETED: needed to set up regular weather updates after device started.
