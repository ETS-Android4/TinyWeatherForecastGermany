<p>Weather forecast with widgets for up to 10 days, based on open data from the Deutscher Wetterdienst (DWD).</p>
<p>Also displays weather warnings issued by the DWD for Germany.</p>
<p>A forecast ist available for over 5000 stations in Germany and worldwide.</p><ul>
<li>detailed weather forecast</li>
<li>slim design</li>
<li>supports wearables through Gadgetbridge</li></ul>

<p><b>Permissions:</b></b><ul>
<li>INTERNET: needed to access the open data from the Deutscher Wetterdienst (DWD).</li>
<li>FOREGROUND_SERVICE: needed to be able to update weather data regularly.</li>
<li>RECEIVE_BOOT COMPLETED: needed to set up regular weather updates after device started.</li></ul>
